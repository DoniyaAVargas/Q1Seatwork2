Characters in Noli me tangere

Maria Clara Maria Clara is described to be everybody's idol,growing up among smiles and loves. Although Noli only touches upon her briefly,she is playful,exchanging bantering with ibarra as well as expressing jealous possession when talking about him to her friends. She is very kind and considerate.
Crisostomo Ibarra A rich mestizo who returned from the Philippines after seven years of studying in Europe, Ibarra is smart, respectful, and very sharp-witted. The priests of San Diego speak highly of Ibarra for his highly liberal education and connections, Considered to marry Maria Clara.
Don Rafael Ibarra Crisóstomo Ibarra’s father. A critic of the corrupt practices of the Spanish friars, he earns the ire of the vitriolic Father Dámaso, who accuses him of sedition and heresy. He dies in prison before his name can be cleared.
Padre Damaso Padre Dámaso an arrogant and selfish priest who, despite having lived amongst Filipinos and hearing their confessions for over twenty years, is barely able to speak or understand Tagalog, the country’s native language. A shameless loudmouth, narcissist and he is unafraid of slandering nonreligious citizens who he thinks undermine his power.
Padre Salvi A manipulative priest that represents the clergy during the time who disguise the yearnings of the flesh under the authority of the Church to get what they want.
